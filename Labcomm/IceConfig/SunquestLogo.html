The tree view to the left displays a list of configurable items. Click the '+' icon to expand and the '-' to collapse.

Clicking on an item will show the relevant list of properties and any value may be subsequently amended.

To add a sub-item, right click on the sub-item header and select add.

To delete a category (where this is allowed), right click on the specific category in the tree view and select 'Delete'

Click the 'OK' button to permanently save any changes. Click 'Cancel' to discard the updates.
